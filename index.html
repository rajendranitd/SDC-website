ABOUT US

Subhadip Das Construction (SDC) delivers high-quality Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) services across Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Infrastructure and Industrial sectors. We specialize in execution of complex fabrication, erection, commissioning and maintenance projects with safety, quality and reliability.

OUR CORE SERVICES

PIPING / PIPELINE PROJECTS

We provide engineering, piping, and pipeline construction services that meet and exceed customer expectations. Our services cover construction of pipelines, fabrication, erection, hydro testing, non-destructive testing (NDT), commissioning & valves, on-line instruments erection and testing.

ROOF DRAIN SYSTEM

SDC Roof Drain System is designed to provide a superior solution to your floating roof tank drainage needs. Our reliable roof drain system ensures effective and safe roof drainage with maintenance-free and worry-free operation.

STRUCTURAL STEEL, TRUSS, SHED FABRICATION & ERECTION

We are a trusted builder of steel truss structures and industrial sheds. Our experienced team fabricates, erects and commissions structural sheds with sheeting safely, within budget and on schedule.

BLASTING & INDUSTRIAL PAINTING

We perform grit/copper slag blasting and industrial airless painting using standard quality paints. Our finishing provides high durability and corrosion-resistance in industrial conditions.

CIVIL WORKS

We execute RCC foundations, industrial floors, drains, boundary works, approach roads, retaining walls, and structural concrete works necessary for industrial and infrastructure projects.

ELECTRICAL WORKS

We execute HT/LT cabling, industrial and plant wiring, earthing system installation, cable tray installation, electrical panel installation, testing and commissioning.

SDC'S KEY STRENGTHS

Engineering, Procurement and Fabrication services delivered by skilled multi-discipline teams using latest modeling technologies. We maintain strong workforce capability including welders, riggers and fabrication professionals ensuring safe and high-quality execution.

QUALITY & SAFETY
